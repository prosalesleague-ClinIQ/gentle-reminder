Validation of the Gentle Reminder Cognitive Monitoring Platform Against Standardized Neuropsychological Assessments in Elderly Adults with Mild-to-Moderate Dementia

## 3. WHY YOU ARE BEING ASKED TO PARTICIPATE

You are being invited to join a research study because your clinician has diagnosed you with mild-to-moderate dementia and you are 65 years of age or older. This study is testing whether a cognitive monitoring app called **Gentle Reminder** produces scores that agree with standard paper-and-pencil cognitive tests (MMSE and MoCA) that your care team already uses.

This is a **research study**. Participating is completely voluntary. Please read this form carefully, ask any questions, and take the form home if you wish to discuss it with family before deciding.

## 4. WHY THIS RESEARCH IS BEING DONE

Current cognitive assessments (MMSE, MoCA) are given in-clinic every few months. Between visits, families and clinicians have limited information about how a person's thinking is changing day to day. The Gentle Reminder app gives short brain-training exercises on a tablet and calculates a score that is meant to track the same kinds of thinking skills that MMSE and MoCA measure.

The purpose of this study is to find out **whether Gentle Reminder's score is a good match for MMSE/MoCA** and whether it can help detect meaningful cognitive decline earlier than standard in-clinic testing alone.

## 5. WHAT WILL HAPPEN IF YOU JOIN

If you agree to participate, the study will last **6 months**. During that time:

### At the first visit (about 90 minutes)
- We will ask you questions about your medical history and daily routine.
- A trained study staff member will give you the **MMSE** and **MoCA** cognitive tests (paper-and-pencil, about 30 minutes total).
- We will help you and your caregiver set up the Gentle Reminder app on a tablet (provided at no cost if you don't own one).
- You will complete a first Gentle Reminder session (about 15 minutes) with us present.
- We will ask you to complete a short **System Usability Scale** survey (10 questions).

### Between visits (Weeks 1 through 24)
- Use the Gentle Reminder app **at least 3 times per week** (each session takes 5–15 minutes).
- Medication reminders will be active on the app according to your current prescription schedule.
- Your caregiver will receive alerts through the app based on settings you choose together.
- **You may stop using the app at any time, for any length of time, without penalty.**

### At follow-up visits (Month 1, Month 3, Month 6)
- Repeat MMSE (and MoCA at Months 3 and 6).
- Complete one Gentle Reminder session with us present.
- Complete the usability survey and a caregiver satisfaction survey.
- At Month 6, we will also do a short **exit interview** (about 15 minutes).

Total time commitment over 6 months: approximately **6 hours** of study visits, plus about 30–45 minutes per week of Gentle Reminder usage.

## 6. RISKS AND DISCOMFORTS

This is an **observational study**. We do not change your medical care in any way. Risks are minimal:

- **Cognitive testing fatigue.** MMSE and MoCA are short tests, but some participants find cognitive testing tiring or frustrating. You may take breaks. You may refuse any individual question.
- **Privacy risk.** Study data includes information about your cognitive performance, medication schedule, sleep timing, and daily routine. We protect this information using industry-standard encryption and HIPAA-compliant storage (see Section 8).
- **Device usability.** The app runs on a touchscreen tablet. Some participants find touchscreens difficult. Study staff will help you adjust display size, brightness, and notification volume. If you are unable to use the tablet comfortably after training, we will withdraw you from the study (your clinical care is not affected).
- **Emotional discomfort.** Being asked about memory difficulties can be upsetting. You may stop at any time. Study staff are trained to respond with empathy and can help connect you to support resources.

**There are no known physical risks** from using the Gentle Reminder app. The app has completed safety testing under ISO 14971 (risk management for medical devices).

## 7. BENEFITS

**You may not receive any direct benefit** from participating. Any insights from the study are for future patients and researchers.

Indirect benefits may include:
- You will receive complementary cognitive exercises during the 6-month study period.
- Your caregiver will receive cognitive status summaries they can share with your care team.
- You are contributing to research that may improve early detection of dementia progression for future patients.

## 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

Your privacy is protected under the **Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)** and **45 CFR Part 46** (federal research protections).

- Your study data will be labeled with a **study ID**, not your name. The list that connects study IDs to names is stored separately, encrypted, and accessible only to the PI and study coordinator.
- Gentle Reminder, Inc. (the company that makes the app) will receive **de-identified** data only — they will never see your name, address, or medical record number.
- Your data will be stored for **25 years** after the study ends (required for FDA-regulated medical device research).
- Your name will **never appear** in any publication, presentation, or report from this study.
- Under very limited circumstances, regulators (FDA, Office for Human Research Protections, the IRB) may review study records as part of their oversight duties. They are legally bound to maintain confidentiality.

**What we will NOT do:**
- We will never sell your data.
- We will never share your data with advertisers or commercial partners.
- We will never use your data for marketing.

## 9. COMPENSATION

- **Study tablet:** Provided at no cost during the study. You may keep it at the end of the study if you complete all 4 study visits.
- **Travel reimbursement:** Up to $25 per study visit (Month 0, 1, 3, 6) = up to $100 total.
- **Time compensation:** $50 gift card at Month 3 and Month 6 (total $100) for completing survey and exit interview.

Total maximum compensation: **$200 in gift cards + one tablet (value approx. $200) + travel reimbursement**.

Compensation is prorated — you receive the portion for visits you complete, even if you withdraw before Month 6.

## 10. WHAT HAPPENS IF YOU ARE INJURED BY PARTICIPATING

This is an observational study; we expect no injuries. In the unlikely event you are injured as a direct result of study participation, the PI will help you access medical care. Medical care for study-related injuries will be billed to you or your insurance in the usual manner. **Gentle Reminder, Inc., [Institution], and the PI do not provide free medical care for research-related injuries.** This does not waive any legal rights you have.

## 11. VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION AND WITHDRAWAL

- **Participation is completely voluntary.** You may refuse to participate.
- **You may stop participating at any time, for any reason, without penalty.** Your clinical care will not be affected.
- If you withdraw, we will ask (but not require) whether we may keep the data already collected about you, to preserve the scientific integrity of the study.
- The PI may withdraw you from the study if: (a) continuing would not be safe for you, (b) you are unable to complete study procedures, or (c) the study is stopped for other reasons.

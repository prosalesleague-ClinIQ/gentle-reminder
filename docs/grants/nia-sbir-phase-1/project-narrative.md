# PROJECT NARRATIVE

*(2-3 sentences, plain-language, for lay audiences per NIH instructions.)*

---

Over 55 million people live with dementia globally, yet standard cognitive assessment tools produce pass/fail feedback that causes anxiety, agitation, and session abandonment — harming the very patients they are meant to help. Gentle Reminder is a software-based medical device that uses 23 novel algorithmic innovations, including a three-state positive-only feedback system, to deliver clinically valid cognitive assessment and digital biomarker tracking for dementia patients without the emotional harm of traditional tests. Successful validation in this Phase I study will establish the platform as the first dementia-safe alternative to standard cognitive instruments, enabling improved clinical care, accelerated pharmaceutical trials for anti-amyloid therapies, and reduced burden on caregivers.

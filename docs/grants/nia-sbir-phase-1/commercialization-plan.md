## 2. VALUE PROPOSITION

Gentle Reminder is the first clinical-grade cognitive assessment and digital biomarker platform designed from the algorithm up for dementia populations. It delivers:

- **For patients:** A dementia-safe cognitive experience that never communicates failure, increasing engagement and reducing anxiety documented in standard assessments.
- **For clinicians:** Granular cognitive data equivalent to MMSE/MoCA/ADAS-Cog, plus continuous digital biomarkers that standard instruments cannot produce.
- **For caregivers:** Real-time patient state visibility and clinical alerts, reducing burden (target ≥20% ZBI reduction).
- **For pharma:** Scalable digital endpoint for anti-amyloid therapy trials and post-market surveillance.
- **For payers:** Early-decline detection enabling risk stratification and cost management.

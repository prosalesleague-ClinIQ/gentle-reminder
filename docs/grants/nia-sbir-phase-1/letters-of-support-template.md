# LETTERS OF SUPPORT

For NIH SBIR Phase I, 3-5 letters of support strengthen the application significantly. Aim for:

1. **Academic clinical collaborator** (subaward PI — institutional letter)
2. **Memory care facility executive** (validates commercial adoption interest)
3. **Industry advisor** (FDA SaMD regulatory expert)
4. **Patient advocacy organization** (optional; Alzheimer's Association or BrightFocus letter of encouragement)
5. **Technology / biomarker expert** (optional)

Below are template letters. Adapt with specific institutional letterhead and signer information.

---

## HOW TO ASSEMBLE THE LETTERS

1. **Send your outreach to each potential letter-writer ≥6 weeks before submission deadline.**
2. Use the templates as starting drafts; each letter-writer should personalize.
3. Collect PDFs on institutional letterhead with physical or electronic signature.
4. Combine all letters into a single PDF and upload to the "Letters of Support" attachment in NIH Assist or Workspace.

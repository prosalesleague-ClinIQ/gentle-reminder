# BUDGET JUSTIFICATION

**Total Direct + Indirect Request:** $275,000 over 12 months
**Small Business Concern F&A Rate:** Negotiated federal rate OR 40% (default for SBIR firms without negotiated rate)

---

## A. PERSONNEL

### A.1 Principal Investigator (Founder/CEO)
- **% Effort:** 30% (3.6 calendar months)
- **Salary:** $140,000 annualized × 30% = $42,000
- **Fringe Benefits:** 25% × $42,000 = $10,500
- **Subtotal:** $52,500

**Justification:** PI leads overall project direction, serves as technical lead for algorithm refinement in response to Phase I data, represents project in clinical partner meetings, and coordinates Aim 1-3 execution. 30% effort is consistent with NIH SBIR Phase I norms for founder-PIs and ensures substantial, dedicated focus while preserving capacity for concurrent seed fundraising.

### A.2 Co-Investigator / Software Engineer
- **% Effort:** 50% (6 calendar months)
- **Salary:** $130,000 annualized × 50% = $65,000
- **Fringe Benefits:** 25% × $65,000 = $16,250
- **Subtotal:** $81,250

**Justification:** Co-Investigator leads platform integration with research study data capture, ensures production-grade quality for data flowing to clinical analysis, implements any platform modifications required by study protocol, and maintains 21 CFR Part 11 compliance of all data. 50% effort reflects the engineering-intensive nature of Aim 2 biomarker data collection and analysis.

### A.3 Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC)
- **% Effort:** 25% (3 calendar months)
- **Salary:** $70,000 annualized × 25% = $17,500
- **Fringe Benefits:** 25% × $17,500 = $4,375
- **Subtotal:** $21,875

**Justification:** CRC manages patient enrollment coordination, IRB submission and amendments, scheduling across the clinical site, data entry quality checks, and caregiver-facing logistics for Aim 3. 25% effort is adequate for a 60-patient single-site Phase I study.

**PERSONNEL TOTAL: $155,625**

---

## B. CONSULTANT COSTS

### B.1 Biostatistician
- **Cost:** $10,000 total across project ($200/hour × 50 hours)

**Justification:** Independent biostatistician reviews the Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP), conducts interim analyses (Aim 1 at 30 patients), and performs final analyses for all three aims including correlation analyses, paired t-tests, and ML model validation for Aim 2. Independence strengthens rigor and reproducibility per NIH guidelines.

### B.2 Regulatory Consultant (FDA SaMD)
- **Cost:** $5,000 total (10 hours × $500/hour)

**Justification:** FDA consultant provides guidance on Phase I data formatting to support downstream 510(k) submission, ensures that data capture from Aim 1 meets substantial equivalence evidentiary requirements, and pre-screens the final Phase I data package.

**CONSULTANT TOTAL: $15,000**

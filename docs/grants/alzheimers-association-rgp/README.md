# Alzheimer's Association — Research Grants Program (RGP)

**Program:** Research Grants Program — New Investigator Track
**Funder:** Alzheimer's Association (US largest AD nonprofit)
**Award:** $150,000 over 1-3 years (varies by track)
**Deadlines:** Multiple cycles annually (check https://www.alz.org/research/for_researchers/grants)
**Eligibility:** US and international investigators; PI at research institution or industry with academic collaboration
**URL:** https://www.alz.org/research/for_researchers/grants

## Strategic Fit

The Alzheimer's Association funds:
- **AARG (Alzheimer's Association Research Grant)** — $150K for 1-2 years; new investigators
- **AARF (Fellowship)** — Junior faculty
- **AARG-NTF (Non-Traditional Focus)** — $150K; technology, platforms, assessment tools ← **best fit for Gentle Reminder**
- **Part the Cloud** — $1M+ for clinical trials (later-stage)

We are targeting AARG-NTF which specifically funds "novel approaches that do not fit conventional research categories."

## Why Gentle Reminder Fits AARG-NTF

AARG-NTF is designed for:
- New methodological approaches
- Platform and tool development
- Assessment validation studies
- Industry-academic partnerships

Our 23-patent platform with dementia-safe cognitive assessment is a textbook fit for "novel approach that doesn't fit conventional NIH research categories."

## Submission Strategy

### Differentiation from SBIR + BrightFocus
- **SBIR focus:** FDA 510(k) pathway, clinical validation for commercialization
- **BrightFocus focus:** Longitudinal biomarker tracking with academic rigor
- **Alzheimer's Association focus:** Caregiver burden reduction + real-world patient experience (fits AARG-NTF "patient-centered research")

### Recommended Approach
Position this grant around **Aim 3** from SBIR (caregiver burden reduction) and real-world deployment feasibility in memory care facilities. The Alzheimer's Association is particularly interested in patient + caregiver outcomes, which is where our platform uniquely contributes.

### Partnership Angle
Alzheimer's Association has formal research partnerships with Biogen, Eisai, Lilly. A successful grant positions us for introduction to their pharma research network.

## Likelihood Assessment

- AARG-NTF funds ~10-15 grants per cycle
- 10-15% funding rate
- Our combination of "novel approach + real-world deployment + patient-centered outcomes" is favorable
- Strong if: clear caregiver burden angle, letter of support from Alzheimer's Association-connected researcher

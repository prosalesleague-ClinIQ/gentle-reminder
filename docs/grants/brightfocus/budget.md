## BUDGET NOTES

- Personnel effort allocation assumes shared infrastructure with a complementary NIA SBIR Phase I / Phase II project. If SBIR is awarded, BrightFocus funds cover incremental 3-year longitudinal follow-up beyond SBIR's 12-month horizon.
- Academic PI subaward will be structured with home institution's federal cost-reimbursement requirements.
- Participant compensation of $200 per patient is consistent with IRB ethical guidelines for longitudinal studies at our partner sites.
- Cloud infrastructure costs are incremental to production platform (which is funded by seed capital independent of this grant).
- No equipment purchases requested (platform is already deployed; iPads are consumable supplies for study sites).

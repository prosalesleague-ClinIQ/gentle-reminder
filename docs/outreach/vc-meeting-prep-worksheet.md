# VC Meeting Prep Worksheet

**Document ID:** VCP-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-04-20
**Purpose:** Fill in once per VC, 48-72 hours before any scheduled meeting. Satisfies the `fund-research`, `partner-research`, `references-prep`, and `competing-offers` items in the Before VC Meeting checklist.

---

## Usage

1. Copy this template into a note per VC (GHL contact note, shared doc, or `docs/outreach/meetings/YYYY-MM-DD-[firm].md`)
2. Fill in fields from public sources (firm site, Crunchbase, LinkedIn, portfolio news, partner tweets)
3. Review with co-founder or advisor before the meeting
4. Attach to GHL contact record
5. Post-meeting: add "What they asked + what surprised me" section for the next-meeting prep

Budget: 60-90 minutes per VC for first meeting. Repeat VCs need only a 15-minute refresh.

## Red flags to catch before a meeting

- Partner's fund just raised Fund N+1 but is still deploying from Fund N — they may be slower than they signal
- Partner recently joined from another firm — may have no check authority yet (or be actively deploying to prove themselves)
- Portfolio has an obvious conflict (competing company) we missed
- Firm has not led a deal in our sector in last 3 years — can't be a lead, only a follower
- Partner has active board obligations consuming > 8 seats — bandwidth risk
- Fund is nominally "digital health" but has deployed < 20% into the sector this cycle — thesis mismatch

If any red flag: adjust the ask (co-invest vs lead, or politely de-prioritize the meeting).

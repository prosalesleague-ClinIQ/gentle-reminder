## Specific Aims Templates

### Template 1: NIA SBIR Phase I — Cognitive Assessment Validation

**Project Title:** Validation of a Novel Non-Negative-Feedback Cognitive Assessment Platform for Early Alzheimer's Disease Detection

**Specific Aims:**

**Aim 1:** Validate the Gentle Feedback Scoring System against standard cognitive tests (MMSE, MoCA, ADAS-Cog) in a 60-patient cohort.
- Hypothesis: Our system demonstrates ≥ 0.80 correlation with MMSE while reducing test-induced anxiety by ≥ 30% (measured via State-Trait Anxiety Inventory).
- Outcomes: concurrent validity metrics, patient-reported anxiety, session completion rates, clinician-rated clinical utility.

**Aim 2:** Characterize the relationship between composite digital biomarkers and short-term cognitive decline.
- Hypothesis: Our 5-analyzer composite score at baseline predicts 6-month MMSE change with Pearson r ≥ 0.65.
- Outcomes: biomarker time series, correlations with clinical decline, ROC analysis.

**Aim 3:** Establish usability and acceptability for patients with mild-to-moderate Alzheimer's.
- Hypothesis: ≥ 80% completion rate for recommended session cadence over 3 months.
- Outcomes: System Usability Scale, retention curves, caregiver satisfaction (Zarit Burden Interview change).

**Project Title:** Longitudinal Validation of Digital Biomarkers for Alzheimer's Disease Progression

(Requires academic PI; typically 5 years; Phase II-like scale)

## Submission Process Checklist

For each grant submission:

- [ ] Register in Grants.gov (one-time)
- [ ] Obtain DUNS / UEI number
- [ ] Register in SAM.gov
- [ ] Register in eRA Commons (for NIH)
- [ ] Complete Small Business Concern (SBC) Registry for SBIR
- [ ] Draft Specific Aims (page 1)
- [ ] Draft Research Plan (6 pages for SBIR Phase I)
- [ ] Draft Biosketches for Key Personnel (5 pages each)
- [ ] Obtain Letters of Support (3-5 recommended)
- [ ] Draft Budget Justification
- [ ] Compile Human Subjects documents (if applicable)
- [ ] Draft Data Sharing Plan
- [ ] Complete SBIR-specific forms (Foreign Work, Commercialization History, Technical Abstract)
- [ ] Submit 1 week before deadline (avoid technical issues)

- **Cost:** $1,000-$3,000 per IP × 5 = $5,000-$15,000
- **Provider:** Cardinal IP, Clarivate, BCC

### International Strategy Decision
- **PCT (Patent Cooperation Treaty):** Single application filed within 12 months preserves priority in 150+ countries
  - Filing fee: $4,000-$6,000
  - Search fee: $2,000-$3,000
  - 30-month window to enter national phase
- **Direct Paris Convention filing:** 12-month window to file separately in each country (more expensive for multiple countries)

**Recommendation:** File PCT for Tier 1 IPs at month 12. Allow other provisionals to lapse or convert to US-only non-provisional.

## Decision Framework: Patent vs. Trade Secret

File patent when:
- ✅ Innovation is detectable in shipped product
- ✅ Competitors could easily reverse-engineer
- ✅ Licensing revenue is feasible
- ✅ FDA submission requires disclosure anyway

Keep as trade secret when:
- ❌ Specific numerical parameters (70-85% comfort zone, keyword dictionaries)
- ❌ Training data compositions
- ❌ Internal process optimizations
- ❌ Difficult to reverse-engineer

**Our strategy:** Patents cover the architectural innovations; trade secrets cover the specific numerical calibrations.

# Strategic Partner Outreach Playbook

## Partnership Models

### 1. Field-of-Use License
**Example:** Biogen licenses GR-03 (Dementia-Adapted Spaced Repetition) for use in a Leqembi companion app. Non-exclusive. Field of use: "anti-amyloid therapy companion software for Alzheimer\'s disease."

- Upfront: $500K-$2M
- Milestone: $1M on first commercial sale
- Royalty: 6-8% of Net Sales
- Minimum annual royalty: $250K
- Term: Patent life + 5 years

### 2. Co-Development Agreement
**Example:** Philips Healthcare co-develops a remote monitoring bundle combining our Composite Biomarker Engine (GR-07) with their wearable data streams.

- Shared R&D investment (typically 60/40 larger party / startup)
- Co-exclusive commercialization rights
- Revenue share based on contribution
- Joint IP for jointly-developed improvements

### 3. Platform Acquisition
**Example:** UnitedHealth Group acquires Gentle Reminder for $50M-$200M to integrate across Medicare Advantage population.

- Full platform + IP portfolio + team
- Retention packages for key engineers
- Integration with existing Optum product lines

## Outreach Sequence

### Step 1 — Identify Decision Maker
For each target:
- **Pharma:** Head of Digital Health, Chief Digital Officer, VP Business Development — Neurology
- **MedTech:** VP Business Development, Head of Partnerships, Chief Digital Officer
- **Payers:** Chief Medical Officer, VP Clinical Strategy, Medicare Advantage product lead
- **Care Operators:** Chief Innovation Officer, VP Clinical Operations

LinkedIn + PitchBook for specific names.

### Step 2 — Cold / Warm Outreach

**Cold email template (pharma):**
```
Subject: 23 patents for Alzheimer's cognitive support — field-of-use license opportunity

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out because [Company]'s Alzheimer's portfolio needs what
my team has built: a 23-patent platform for dementia cognitive
assessment, AI companion, and biomarkers — specifically designed
for patients on anti-amyloid therapy.

We've filed provisional patents covering:
- The only zero-negative-feedback cognitive assessment system
- Dementia-adapted spaced repetition (memory maintenance)
- Multimodal AI classification (confusion, agitation, sundowning)
- FDA SaMD compliance framework

Three partnership models are on the table:
1. Field-of-use license for [Leqembi / Kisunla] companion use
2. Co-development of outcome tracking (real-world evidence)
3. Acquisition of the full platform

We're happy to walk through the IP portfolio under NDA.
Brief overview: gentle-reminder.health

Could we schedule 30 min this month?

Best,
[Founder Name]
```

### Step 3 — NDA Execution

Use a mutual NDA template. Key terms:
- 3-year term
- Mutual confidentiality
- Specific carve-outs for independently developed information
- Residual clause (knowledge retained in memory)
- Return/destroy upon termination

### Step 4 — IP Portfolio Walk-Through (60-90 min)

After NDA, share:
- Detailed IP descriptions (all 23)
- Patent claim previews
- Codebase architecture
- FDA regulatory status
- Clinical validation plans

### Step 5 — Partnership Term Sheet

Typical timeline: 8-16 weeks from initial meeting to term sheet.

## Acquisition Memorandum

For full platform acquisitions, prepare:

1. **Executive Summary** (1 page)
2. **Business Overview** (market, product, traction)
3. **IP Portfolio Detail** (23 IPs with valuations)
4. **Team & Organization** (cap table, key employees)
5. **Technology Architecture** (code, infrastructure, data)
6. **Clinical/Regulatory** (FDA status, validation data)
7. **Financials** (revenue, burn rate, projections)
8. **Contracts & Customers** (pipeline, agreements)
9. **Risks** (competitive, regulatory, market)
10. **Valuation & Structure** (ask, structure preferences)

Valuation range: $50M-$200M based on:
- IP portfolio value: $22M-$56M
- Platform code value: $2.5M-$5M
- FDA pathway progress: $10M-$30M
- Team retention: $5M-$15M
- Strategic fit premium: 2x-4x

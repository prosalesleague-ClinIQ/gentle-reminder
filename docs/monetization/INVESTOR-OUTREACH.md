## Outreach Sequence

### Week 1 — Initial Contact
- Cold email (above template) OR warm intro request via LinkedIn
- If no response within 5 business days, send follow-up #1 with different angle

### Week 2 — Follow-up #1
Angle: news hook (Leqembi launch update, NIH grant announcement, competitor raise)

### Week 3 — Follow-up #2
Angle: specific portfolio relevance (reference partner's previous investment)

### Week 4 — Break / Redirect
If still no response, move on or redirect to different partner at same fund.

## Meeting Preparation

### First Meeting (30 min)
Goal: Qualify mutual interest. Do NOT over-explain.

- 5 min: Your intro + market context
- 5 min: IP portfolio highlights (top 3-5 patents)
- 10 min: Platform demo / traction / team
- 5 min: Ask + use of funds
- 5 min: Their questions

### Second Meeting (60 min)
Goal: Deep-dive on unit economics, FDA strategy, competitive moat.

- Financial model walk-through
- Clinical validation plan
- Partnership pipeline
- Competitive analysis

### Partner Meeting / Full Partnership
Goal: Get to term sheet.

- Full due diligence package
- Customer references (as available)
- Clinical advisor introductions

## Common Questions (Prepare Answers)

### Market
- "How big is the ACTUAL addressable market?" — $450M (memory care facilities + 510(k)-approved DTx prescriptions)
- "Isn't Biogen's Leqembi enough?" — Leqembi slows disease 27% but doesn't address daily care. We complement pharma, we don't compete.

### Product
- "Why hasn't someone built this?" — Required crossing 4 domains: clinical, ML, UX, FDA. Most startups focus on one.
- "What's your moat?" — 23 patents, architectural lock-in, FDA pathway years ahead.

### Team
- "Why are YOU the team to do this?" — [Prepare specific answer based on founders]
- "Who's your clinical advisor?" — [Fill with actual names once recruited]

### Financial
- "Why $5M and not $10M?" — Tight use of funds, 12-month runway to Series A, don't over-dilute.
- "When profitable?" — Y4 at $38M ARR with positive unit economics.

### Competitive
- "How do you beat Akili / Cogstate / Linus?" — They're general-purpose cognitive platforms. We're dementia-specific from the algorithm up. Our 23 patents architecturally block them from our market.

### FDA
- "How long to 510(k)?" — 12-18 months from submission. We can submit ~Month 12 of seed (predicate + substantial equivalence).
- "What if FDA rejects?" — De Novo pathway backup. Also defensive publication protects commercial path even without cleared device for non-DTx uses.

## Rejection Handling

**"Not right for us."** — Ask: "Who in your network might be?" Get referrals.

**"Come back when you have more traction."** — Define specific traction. Get back on their calendar in 2-3 months with that traction.

**"Team is too small."** — Ask: "What role would need to be filled for you to reconsider?" Get specific.

**"Too early."** — Ask: "What milestone would move us to the right stage for you?"

**No response after 3 touches** — Move on. Do not waste energy chasing disengaged funds.
